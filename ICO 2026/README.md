Location: Hammamet, Tunisia

in the heart of North Africa.

---

#### **Note: All of the writeups in this directory are written by AI.**
The purpose of these writeups is primarily for my own learning, as well as my contestants, and to prepare for next year, and are not necessarily a reflection of my own skills (you can look at my other writeups for that). These are not the official writeups, which have not been posted as of 7/2, but rather a collection of the writeups that I asked Claude to generate after the competition was over for the challenges I couldn't solve or wanted to understand better. To be clear, the ICO enforced a restricted AI policy this year which involved limiting access to LLMs during the CTF through a custom website that tracked token usage and only allowed one model.

As for the scripts, some are written entirely by me, some entirely by AI, and some by both. You can probably tell by the quality of my code :P
